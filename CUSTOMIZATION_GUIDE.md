## üé® Customization Options

### Change Theme Colors

All the stat cards use the `radical` theme. You can change this to other themes:

Available themes: `dark`, `radical`, `merko`, `gruvbox`, `tokyonight`, `onedark`, `cobalt`, `synthwave`, `highcontrast`, `dracula`, and more.

Just replace `theme=radical` with your preferred theme in the URLs.

### Adjust Banner Text

The header banner can be customized in line 2:
- Change `text=Alwin%20Saji` to your name (use %20 for spaces)
- Modify colors, animations, and size

Visit: https://github.com/kyechan99/capsule-render for more options

### Hide Specific Stats

You can hide specific stats from the GitHub stats card by adding parameters:
- `&hide=stars,commits,prs,issues`
- `&hide_rank=true`

Example:
```markdown
![Stats](https://github-readme-stats.vercel.app/api?username=Alwin-Saji&show_icons=true&theme=radical&hide=stars&hide_rank=true)
```
